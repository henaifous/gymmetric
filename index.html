About me

Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Gymmetric is designed with a privacy-first approach and keeps your workout data centered on your device.

📱 Data Storage

All workout data — routines, sessions, sets, and exercise logs — is stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. Nothing is sent to external servers.

🚫 No Account Required

Gymmetric does not require you to create an account, provide an email, or sign in with any service. You can use the full app anonymously.

📡 Network Usage

The app may load exercise demonstration images from the internet. These requests do not include any personal data, workout history, or device identifiers.

💳 In-App Purchases

Subscriptions are handled entirely through Apple's StoreKit framework. Payment information is managed by Apple — Gymmetric never sees or stores your payment details.

📊 Analytics & Crash Reporting

Gymmetric uses Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics to understand how the app is used and to fix crashes. These collect anonymous usage events and crash logs — no personal workout data or identifiable information is included. No data is shared with advertisers or data brokers.

🗑️ Data Deletion

Since all data is stored on-device, deleting the app removes all your data completely. There is nothing stored on our servers to delete.
